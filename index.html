Who are we?

We are Stream, a digital movement redefining how Africans experience and earn from entertainment. Built on the belief that your digital presence should be rewarding, Stream connects people, creativity, and opportunity in one continuous flow.

Through partnerships with artists, record labels, movie studios, and content distributors, Stream serves as a bridge between creators and the audience. We help them reach real streamers and in return, our users (the Streamers) are rewarded for every collaboration they contribute to.

Stream transforms ordinary digital engagement into real value. Every time you collaborate, you’re not just partnering, you’re participating in a new digital economy where creativity and rewards flow together.
