About Stream Africa

Redefining the Digital Economy across the continent.

Who We Are

Stream Africa is the premier digital rewards platform connecting content creators with a highly engaged audience. Established with the vision of making digital time valuable, we have created an ecosystem where users are rewarded for their attention and creators find their true audience.

We facilitate collaboration between users and partners in the music, film, and digital marketing industries. Through partnerships with artists, record labels, and movie studios, Stream serves as a bridge between creators and the audience.

Our Mission: To democratize the creator economy by ensuring that every interaction—whether it's streaming a song, watching a trailer, or engaging with content—generates tangible value for both the creator and the consumer.

Redefining digital engagement in Africa. Stream connects you to the creators you love and rewards you for your attention.

Disclaimer: Stream Africa is a digital rewards and loyalty platform. We are not an investment scheme. Rewards are based on active participation and completed tasks. Results vary by user activity.
